A Platform for Recruiters to Shortlist Passionate Candidates

We make task of shortlisting candidates easy for recruites by shortlisting strong resumes as per their requirements & show them top resumes to make their job easy to hire the candidate.

How We Process?

  • Step 1

    Firstly, we store the requirements of the recruiter.

  • Step 2

    Then we store the resume details of the Candidates.

  • Step 3

    Then we shortlist the resumes based on the requirements provided by the recruiter.

Steps For Recruiters to Create Job Posting

There are three steps for the Recruiters to create job posting. Here are three steps :-

Step 1

Firstly, Recruiter needs to signup on our platform by filling up basic details like company name, comany email address and password.

Step 2

Then Recruiter needs to fill out the requirements that he/she want into candidate.This requirements helps us to shortlist candidates.

Step 3

Then we generate a one link for your job posting and share a link so that candidates can apply to your job posting.
